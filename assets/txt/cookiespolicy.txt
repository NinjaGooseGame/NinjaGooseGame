1. What Are Cookies
Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow the site to recognize your device, remember your preferences, and improve your browsing experience.

Cookies can be:
• **Session cookies** – Deleted when you close your browser.  
• **Persistent cookies** – Remain stored on your device for a set period or until deleted.  
• **Third-party cookies** – Set by domains other than ours (e.g., Google, Meta, Unity).
